FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by project. A logo takes 2–5 days, a basic website 1–2 weeks, a Shopify store 2–4 weeks, and complex automation systems 4–8 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline upfront before starting.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely! We work with clients across India, the USA, UK, UAE, Australia, and globally. We accept both INR (₹) and USD ($). Communication happens over WhatsApp, email, and video calls — fully remote.
What is the minimum budget for a project?
We start from as low as ₹999 / $10 for smaller tasks like logo design or a single product photoshoot. Larger projects like full Shopify stores or automation systems are custom-quoted based on scope. We always provide transparent pricing before starting.
Do you provide post-launch support?
Yes! All projects include 30 days of post-launch support at no extra cost. Beyond that, we offer ongoing maintenance and growth retainer packages. We're a long-term partner, not a one-time vendor.
How do payments work?
We follow milestone-based payments — typically 50% upfront and 50% on delivery for most projects. For larger projects, we split into 3 milestones. We accept bank transfer, UPI, Razorpay, and PayPal (for international clients).
Can you help if I'm not sure what I need?
That's what we're best at! Just describe your business problem — we'll audit your situation and recommend the right solution. Book a free consultation and we'll map out the exact solution within 24 hours.
